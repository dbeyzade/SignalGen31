# Privacy Policy for SignalGen31

**Last updated:** November 7, 2025

## Introduction

SignalGen31 ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

**We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

SignalGen31 operates entirely on your device and does not:
- Collect personal data
- Track user activity
- Store user information on external servers
- Share data with third parties
- Require user accounts or registration

## Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

### Bluetooth Permission
- **Purpose:** To enable Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) server functionality and connect to Bluetooth devices
- **Data Usage:** Bluetooth connections are established locally on your device. No connection data is transmitted or stored externally.

### Audio Permission
- **Purpose:** To generate and output audio signals
- **Data Usage:** Audio output is processed locally on your device. No audio data is recorded, stored, or transmitted.

## Data Storage

All app data (signal parameters, settings, etc.) is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not backed up to our servers
- Is deleted when you uninstall the app

## Third-Party Services

SignalGen31 does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed as a technical tool and does not target children.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
- **GitHub:** https://github.com/dbeyzade/SignalGen31

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. All app data is stored locally on your device and is under your control.

---

**Summary:** SignalGen31 is a privacy-focused app that does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All functionality operates locally on your device.
